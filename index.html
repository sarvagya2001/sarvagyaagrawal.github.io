Tech stack:
Programming Language used: Python, Javascript, HTML, CSS
Database: PostgreSQL, SQLite3
Framework: Django, Django Rest-API
Deployment: Heroku

COVID19-HELPCARE WEBSITE

I have designed a website to combat the problem of verified sources for COVID 19 patients. Patients can check the list of oxygen suppliers, list of plasma donors who are willing to donate the plasma or patient's can directly post their requirement on the website. Anyone who wants to donate plasma or have information about oxygen supplier can also post their entry on it.
